ABOUT

—
Designing products, systems, and experiences used by millions.

⏱  Reading time: about 2 minutes


I’ve always been drawn to the deeper structure of digital products — not only how they look, but how they work, how they grow, and how they make sense for the people who rely on them.
I’m Mobin Bahrami, a Senior Product Designer with over 15 years of experience across fintech, edtech, and consumer products. Over time, my practice has evolved from visual and interface design into a broader discipline shaped by product strategy, systems thinking, and human-centered design.
What interests me most is complexity: the kind that emerges when products scale, teams grow, platforms multiply, and user needs become increasingly nuanced.
In those environments, design has to do more than create polished screens. It has to create clarity.
Throughout my career, I’ve led design initiatives from discovery and research to interaction design and systems architecture.
I care about products that feel simple, even when the systems behind them are not. For me, design is ultimately about creating meaning through clarity — making products more useful, more intuitive, and more enduring over time.
